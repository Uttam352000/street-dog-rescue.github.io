ABOUT US

SAVE US is a kolkata based NGO registered under trust act in 2016 that has been working for the welfare of stray animals and their rights. Two individual animal lovers Sneha Chatterjee and Uttam Karmakar have been running this shelter cum hospital since 2014. The main objective of our organization is to create a better world for the voiceless friends by spreading awareness among people so that people live in harmony with the less privileged ones and defend their rights.

Our goal is to rescue animals those are ill or injured or animals those are subject to torture and abuse. We provide them relief, treatment adequate care and a safe Refuge. We also work relentlessly for adoption of those who are in need and giving them a safe future. We also try our best to give shelter to senior and differently abled street dogs.

We started our shelter within the small premises of our house only with a few rooms and a small kennel. But with the passing of time and increasing demand of welfare activities, we feel the urgency to expand very soon with a bigger shelter cum rehabilitation center.

We conduct regular and organized ABC programs, proper Vaccination, deworming and feeding drives with an aim that one day there will be no animal going to sleep with a hungry tummy or without a roof above his/her head.
