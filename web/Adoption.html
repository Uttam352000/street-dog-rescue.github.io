Animal Adoption

1.Active Adoption: ARC arranges for adoption of healthy dogs and pups after proper house check and legal paperwork. ARC also believes in maintaining proper healthy relation with adopter even after adoption to get proper updates of the adopted animal.

2.Passive Adoption: In certain cases where active adoption is not possible the kids are put up for passive adoption. Passive adoption allows people to have a pet without taking much physical responsibility.
